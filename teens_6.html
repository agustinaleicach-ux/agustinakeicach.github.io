📝 Grammar Summary

✅ Advanced Conditionals Review

  • Zero: If + Present, Present (universal truths)
  • First: If + Present, will (real future)
  • Second: If + Past, would (unreal present)
  • Third: If + Past Perfect, would have (unreal past)
  • Mixed: If + Past Perfect, would (past → present)

✅ Advanced Modal Verbs

ModalMeaningExample
must haveCertain deduction (past)He must have known the answer.
can't haveImpossible (past)She can't have finished already.
might havePossibility (past)They might have left early.
should haveRegret / criticism (past)You should have told me!

✅ Subjunctive & Formal Language

  • It is essential that he be informed. (formal)
  • I suggest that she reconsider her decision.
  • Were it not for your help, I would have failed.

✅ Hedging & Academic Register

  • It would appear that… / It seems likely that…
  • This tends to suggest… / The evidence points to…
