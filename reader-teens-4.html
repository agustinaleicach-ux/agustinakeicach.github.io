Inspector Jenny Logan is enjoying a day off at Portobello beach when her phone rings. A murderer named Ronnie Campbell has escaped from prison — and a young woman named Morag Mackenzie has been found dead in Holyrood Park. Seven years ago, Morag gave evidence against Ronnie in court. Is this a coincidence? Logan doesn't think so — but the puzzle is more complex than it seems. 🧩🔍
