📖 About the Book

Author
Stephen Rabley
Series
Penguin Readers
Level
Easystarts (200 words)
Setting
🏝️ The Bahamas
Maisie lives in the Bahamas with her family. Her parents run an animal hospital, but they don't have enough money to keep it open. One day, Maisie finds an injured dolphin — and this friendship leads to an incredible discovery that could change everything for her family! 🐬💎

👥 Characters

👧
Maisie
A 13-year-old girl who loves the sea, pop music and swimming.
🐬
Ben
The dolphin. Maisie rescues him and they become best friends.
👴
Grandfather
He goes out in his boat with Maisie. He loves the ocean.
👨‍⚕️
Maisie's Parents
Doctors who run the Freeport Animal Hospital.
😈
Carl Flint
A greedy man who wants to buy the family's land to build a hotel.

🗺️ Story Map

🚤 Beginning: Maisie and her grandfather are in a boat. A big boat hits a dolphin. Maisie rescues the injured dolphin and takes him to the animal hospital.
⚡ Middle: Carl Flint — the boat driver who hit Ben — offers to buy the family's home to build a hotel. Maisie is heartbroken. She is going to lose her home AND her dolphin friend.
💎 End: When Ben recovers and swims back to sea, he leads Maisie to an underwater treasure box! The gold saves the hospital and the family can stay in their home. 🎉
