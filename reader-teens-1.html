Harry Black is a thief. He steals a very expensive painting from an art gallery and hides it inside an old newspaper in his room. But then the recycling truck arrives — and Harry's newspaper disappears with it! Can Harry find his painting before the police find him? Meanwhile, his neighbour Janey spots a crucial clue...
