📰 Student Tips

Starting English: Your First Steps!

Welcome to Kids Starter! This year you'll discover how amazing English is. Here are some great tips to make your learning journey super fun:

🎨 Learn with colours

Use different coloured pens for different words. Write nouns in blue, verbs in red, and adjectives in green. Your brain will remember colours!

🎵 Sing it!

Many of the words in your book have songs. Try singing the alphabet, numbers, and colours every morning. Music helps your memory a lot.

💡 Did you know? Children who practise English for just 10 minutes every day learn twice as fast as those who only study in class!

📖 Your Reader: The Skateboarder

This story is about a boy who loves skateboarding. As you read, try to find all the colours and numbers mentioned in the book — it's like a treasure hunt!

🗣️ Practice at home

  • Say "Good morning!" to your family in English.
  • Count objects you see: "One chair, two chairs…"
  • Name colours when you draw or paint.
  • Ask "What's this?" and answer in English.

🌟 Remember

Making mistakes is totally normal and part of learning. Every English speaker in the world made mistakes when they started. Be brave and speak!
