📰 Student Tips

Teens 2: Building Real English Skills 💪

In Teens 2 the language gets more interesting and complex. You'll explore topics like technology, the environment, health and travel — all things that matter in the real world!

⚡ Present Perfect vs Past Simple

This is the most common mistake at this level. Remember: use Present Perfect for life experiences with no specific time, and Past Simple when you mention when it happened.

  • ✅ I have been to London. (no specific time)
  • ✅ I went to London last summer. (specific time)
💡 Trick: If the sentence has a time expression like yesterday, last week, in 2019, ago → use Past Simple. If it has ever, never, already, just, yet → use Present Perfect!
