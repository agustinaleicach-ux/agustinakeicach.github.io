Jasper is a leprechaun who has a secret pot of gold — hidden at the end of a rainbow! His friend Liam knows about it, but so do the angry villagers who want the gold too. Will the villagers find the treasure? And what is more valuable than gold in the end? 🍀💛

🗺️ Story Map

🧝 Beginning: Jasper the leprechaun lives in Rainbow Land with his friend Liam. The villagers discover Jasper has a pot of gold.
🌈 Middle: The angry villagers chase Jasper, trying to find where he hides his gold. Jasper has a secret map — but will he reveal it?
💛 End: In the end, the villagers discover something more valuable than gold — friendship and happiness. Jasper and Liam celebrate together! 🎉

✍️ Creative Activities

🗺️ Activity 1 – Jasper's Secret Diary

Write a diary entry for Jasper on the day the villagers discover his gold. How does he feel? What does he do? Use Present Simple and adverbs of frequency (always, sometimes, never). Write 5–6 sentences.

🌈 Activity 2 – Design Your Rainbow Land

Imagine YOU live in Rainbow Land. Describe what it looks like using There is / There are and can / can't. What can you do there? What can't you do? Write 6 sentences.

💛 Activity 3 – Something More Valuable Than Gold

The story teaches us that friendship is more valuable than gold. Write about what YOU think is the most important thing in life. Use I think, because, and examples from your life. Write 5–6 sentences.
