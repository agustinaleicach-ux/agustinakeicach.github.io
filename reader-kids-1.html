👑 A kingdom long ago
A beautiful princess loses her golden ball in a pond. A frog finds it and offers to help — but he asks for something in return. The princess makes a promise, but will she keep it? And what happens when a frog is not just a frog? 🐸✨

👥 Characters

👸
The Princess
Beautiful but spoiled. She learns an important lesson.
🐸
The Frog
Clever and kind. He is not what he seems!
👑
The King
The princess's father. He tells her to keep her promise.
🤴
The Prince
A young man under a magic spell. Can he be freed?

🗺️ Story Map

🌊 Beginning: The princess drops her golden ball into the pond. She is very sad.
🤝 Middle: The frog helps her, but she must keep a promise. When the frog comes to her house, she doesn't want to let him in — but the king says she must.
✨ End: The frog is really a prince under a spell! When the princess keeps her promise, the spell is broken and the prince is free. 🎉

✍️ Creative Activities

💌 Activity 1 – The Frog's Letter

Write a letter from the frog to the princess before she finds her ball. He wants to be her friend. Use There is / There are and I have got. Write 4–5 sentences.

0 characters

👑 Activity 2 – A New Fairy Tale Ending

What if the princess never kept her promise? Write a different ending for the story. Use This / That / These / Those and possessive adjectives (my, his, her, their). Write 5–6 sentences.

0 characters

🎨 Activity 3 – Draw and Describe

Imagine you are the prince after the spell is broken. Write a description of how you feel and what you see around you. Use adjectives and There is / There are. Write 5 sentences.
