by Christine Lindop · Oxford Dominoes Quick Starter

📖 About the Book

Author
Christine Lindop
Publisher
Oxford University Press
Series
Dominoes Quick Starter
Genre
🏙️ Contemporary / Sport
Hannah loves watching Owen do amazing skateboard tricks at the skatepark near her house. She wants to skateboard too — but can she do it? Who laughs at her? Who helps her? And can Hannah become a real skateboarder in the end? 🛹

👥 Characters

👧
Hannah
The main character. She loves skateboarding and wants to learn.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Owen
An amazing skateboarder. Hannah admires his tricks.
👦
Evan
Hannah's brother. He asks annoying questions.
🧑
Justin
Hannah's cousin. He helps her practise skateboarding.
👩
Mum & Dad
Hannah's parents. They give her a special birthday present.

🗺️ Story Map

🏠 Beginning: A new skatepark opens near Hannah's house. She sees Owen and falls in love with skateboarding.
⚡ Middle: Hannah tries to skateboard but finds it very hard. Some people laugh at her. She feels sad but doesn't give up.
🎉 End: On her birthday, Hannah gets a skateboard as a present. With Justin's help, she practises and learns. She becomes a real skateboarder!
