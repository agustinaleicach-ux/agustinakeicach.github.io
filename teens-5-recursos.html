📰 Student Tips

Teens 5 — Let's Level Up! 🌟

Teens 5 brings you to upper-intermediate level — the threshold for Cambridge First Certificate (B2). This year's language is sophisticated and you should be proud!

✍️ Writing skills for FCE

At this level, examiners look for variety. Don't just use simple sentences — use inversion, cleft sentences and discourse markers to show your range.

💡 Writing tip: Start one paragraph with an inversion: "Not only does this affect individuals, but it also has wider societal implications." Examiners love it!

📖 Reader: East 43rd Street

This New York thriller is perfect for B2 level. Nathan Marley's complex case will keep you reading. Try to notice advanced vocabulary in context — this is the best way to absorb upper-intermediate words.

🛣️ After Teens 5

Next year you'll take Teens 6, completing Harmonize 5 (Units 5–8). You'll be ready for FCE preparation after that!
