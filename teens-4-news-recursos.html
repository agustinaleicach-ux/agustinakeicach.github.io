Grammar Tip of the Day
Third Conditional in the news: When you read about events that happened differently, practise thinking in Third Conditional. "If the school had banned phones earlier, students would have performed better." Try writing one Third Conditional sentence about each article you read! This is exactly the grammar focus of Teens 4 (Harmonize 4, Units Welcome–7).
