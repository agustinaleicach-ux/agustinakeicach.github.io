Setting

It's Christmas in New York and private investigator Nathan Marley is alone in a bar when a wealthy, mysterious woman enters. The next morning she appears in his office with a strange request: collect a briefcase of jewellery from a locker at Grand Central Station. Marley is suspicious from the start — and he's right to be. The case soon leads to fraud, computer hacking, kidnap, and a web of lies that puts his life in danger. 🗽🌃
