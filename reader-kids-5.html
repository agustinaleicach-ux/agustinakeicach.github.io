A Little Trouble in California

by Richard MacAndrew · Cambridge Experience Readers · Starter Level

📖 About the Book

Author
Richard MacAndrew
Series
Cambridge Experience Readers
Genre
🕵️ Mystery / Adventure
Setting
🌴 California, USA
Andy and Mary are twins on holiday in California with their family. Mary gets a new camera for her birthday and takes photos on the beach — but one photo shows a mysterious man who is very, very angry. Who is this man? Why doesn't he want to be photographed? And are the twins in danger? 📸🕵️

👥 Characters

One of the twins. She gets a new camera and takes mysterious photos.
👦

😠
Darryl
The mysterious man on the beach. He doesn't want to be photographed.
👮
Officer McMahon
A police officer who helps the twins solve the mystery.
👨‍👩‍👧
The Parents
David and Ellen. They are on holiday with the twins in California.

🗺️ Story Map

🎂 Beginning: The Lawson family arrives in California for their holiday. Mary gets a new camera for her birthday and starts taking photos on the beach.
😠 Middle: A man called Darryl shouts at Mary — he doesn't want to be in her photos. The twins notice something strange in the photo. Who is this man and what is he hiding?
🚔 End: Officer McMahon arrives and the mystery is solved. Darryl is a criminal trying to hide his identity. The twins are safe and have the best holiday story to tell! 🎉

✍️ Creative Activities

🎬 Activity 1 – A Different Ending (Past Simple)

What if Officer McMahon never arrived? Write your own ending. What did the twins do? Did Darryl escape? Use Past Simple verbs throughout. Write 5–6 sentences.

🗺️ Activity 2 – Postcard from California (Present Perfect + going to)

You are Andy or Mary. Write a postcard to a friend back home. Tell them what you have done (Present Perfect) and what you are going to do next. Write 6 sentences.
